Who We Are

Bridging Medical Expertise with Patient Comfort

Lillybeauty San Rafael was established with a clear vision: to make advanced aesthetic medicine accessible, safe, and genuinely enjoyable. We are not a day spa with a medical aesthetic add-on. We are a medical practice that happens to feel like a luxury spa.

Every treatment protocol is designed by our medical team. Every device we operate is FDA-cleared. Every practitioner is licensed, certified, and continuously trained on the latest advances in aesthetic medicine.

We serve Sunrise, FL and the greater Broward County area with the same standard of care our founder brought to her dermatology practice — backed now by over 50,000 treatments and 15 years of patient trust.
