LED Facial

Targeted light wavelengths penetrate different depths of the skin to trigger specific cellular responses. Red light stimulates collagen, blue light eliminates acne bacteria, and near-infrared reduces deep inflammation.

From $95 · 30 min
  • Red (630nm): collagen synthesis and anti-aging
  • Blue (415nm): acne bacteria elimination
  • Near-infrared: deep tissue repair and recovery
  • Completely painless and non-invasive
  • Ideal add-on to any facial treatment

  • Instantly smoother skin texture and radiant glow
  • Removes vellus hair for flawless makeup application
  • Enhances absorption of serums and moisturizers
  • Safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin
  • Zero downtime — return to normal activity immediately
