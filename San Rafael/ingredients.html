Ingredients

Every ingredient in our formulations is chosen for its clinical efficacy and safety record.

At Lillybeauty San Rafael, we believe in full transparency. We use only medical-grade, clinically studied actives — no fillers, no unnecessary fragrance, and no compromises on safety or performance.

Brightening

Vitamin C (L-Ascorbic Acid)

A potent antioxidant that inhibits melanin production, neutralizes free radicals, and stimulates collagen synthesis for visibly brighter, firmer skin.

Hydration

Hyaluronic Acid

A naturally occurring humectant that can hold up to 1,000× its weight in water, delivering deep, long-lasting hydration to all skin layers.

Anti-Aging

Retinol (Vitamin A)

The gold standard in anti-aging, retinol accelerates cell turnover, reduces fine lines and wrinkles, and improves skin texture and tone over time.

Firming

Peptide Complexes

Short chains of amino acids that signal the skin to produce more collagen and elastin, resulting in firmer, more resilient skin with improved elasticity.

Brightening

Niacinamide (Vitamin B3)

A versatile active that minimizes pores, regulates sebum, fades hyperpigmentation, and strengthens the skin barrier — suitable for all skin types.

Cellular Repair

Growth Factors

Naturally derived signaling proteins that stimulate cellular regeneration, promote wound healing, and support collagen production at a deep cellular level.

Soothing

Centella Asiatica

A botanical extract with powerful anti-inflammatory and wound-healing properties. Calms redness, soothes irritation, and supports barrier repair.

Acne Control

Salicylic Acid (BHA)

A beta-hydroxy acid that penetrates deep into pores to dissolve excess sebum and dead skin cells, effectively clearing and preventing breakouts.

Nourishing

Rosehip Seed Oil

Rich in essential fatty acids and vitamin A, rosehip oil deeply nourishes the skin, reduces scarring, and improves overall texture and tone.
